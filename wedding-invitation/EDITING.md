# Editing

Open `script.js` to change the wedding date, music start time, and volume.

Open `index.html` to edit invitation wording, time, venue, and map link.

Assets:
- `assets/envelope.jpeg` - opening cover
- `assets/couple.jpeg` - couple introduction
- `assets/card-background.jpeg` - background for invitation slides
- `assets/music.mp3` - background music
- `assets/music-clip.mov` - preferred music clip beginning at the requested lyric

The RSVP form is configured for Netlify Forms. Responses appear under Forms in the Netlify dashboard after deployment.

To receive Gmail notifications in Netlify:
1. Open the deployed site in the Netlify dashboard.
2. Open Forms, then Form notifications.
3. Add an Email notification and enter your Gmail address.

WhatsApp notifications require a separate WhatsApp Business API integration and are not included.
